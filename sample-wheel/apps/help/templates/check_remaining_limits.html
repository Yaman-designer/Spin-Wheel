How do I check remaining limits in my plan?

Validated on 15 Jan 2024 • Last edited on 27 Jun 2025

Overview

Monitoring your remaining usage limits is essential to ensure your campaigns run smoothly. The Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of your current balance, usage statistics, and performance metrics. This guide will help you understand how to check your remaining limits in your plan.

1. Go to the Homepage

  1. Open your web browser and go to https://app.wheelluck.com.
  2. Make sure you are logged in to your account from the top right corner.

2. View the Dashboard Page

  1. After logging in, you'll be automatically directed to the Dashboard page.
  2. You can always access the Dashboard from the left-hand menu.

3. Balance | Today

  1. This box, located at the top of the Dashboard, shows your current daily balance.
  2. Example: 39970 → This number indicates the remaining total usage rights you have.
  3. If you see "Excellent" below the number, it means everything is running smoothly.

4. Awards | This Month

  1. This section shows the total number of rewards given in the current month.
  2. For example, if 79 rewards were given, it indicates the system is actively being used and your credits are decreasing accordingly.
  3. The 'Increase' percentage shows the growth compared to the previous month.
  4. You can change the time period by clicking the filter icon at the top right of the Awards box and selecting options like Today, This Month, or This Year to view reward statistics in detail.

5. Conversion | This Year

  1. This percentage indicates how effectively the system is performing.
  2. It reflects your usage efficiency (e.g., 22.57%).
  3. You can track how well the system is converting interactions into outcomes.
  4. Use the filter icon in the top right corner of the Conversion box to change the time period to Today, This Month, or This Year, and analyze performance by date.

6. Chart Area – Reports

  1. In the center of the Dashboard, graphs show daily statistics for:
    • Initial (initial view)
    • View (total views)
    • Registered (number of users registered)
  2. You can monitor when and how much of your credits have been used by date.

Important Notes

  • If you'd like to see more detailed information about your remaining usage limits, click the "Upgrade Plan" button located at the bottom left corner of the screen.
  • Right above this button, you can also view your current plan.
  • Regularly monitoring your balance helps you avoid unexpected interruptions in your campaigns.
  • The dashboard updates in real-time, so you can see your current usage at any moment.
  • If your balance is running low, consider upgrading your plan to ensure uninterrupted service.

Understanding Your Limits

  • Daily Balance: Shows your remaining credits for the current day. This number decreases as you use the system.
  • Monthly Rewards: Tracks how many rewards have been given this month. Higher numbers indicate more credit usage.
  • Conversion Rate: Measures the efficiency of your campaigns. Higher conversion rates mean better use of your credits.
  • Chart Statistics: Visual representation of your usage patterns over time, helping you identify trends and optimize your campaigns.
