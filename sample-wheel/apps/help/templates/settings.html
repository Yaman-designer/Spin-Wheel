Account Settings Overview

The Account Settings section is your central hub to manage your profile information and security options. You can customize your experience and protect your account from a single place.

Settings Categories

  • Change Email Address: Update your contact information
  • Request Account Deletion: Permanently remove your account
  • Security Settings: Manage passwords and two-factor authentication

Profile Management

Update your personal information:

  1. Access Account Settings from your dashboard
  2. Navigate to "Profile Information"
  3. Update your details as needed
  4. Save changes to apply updates

Security Features

Protect your account with:

  • Strong password requirements
  • Two-factor authentication
  • Email notifications for security-sensitive changes
  • Session management
