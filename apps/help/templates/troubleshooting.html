If you're experiencing problems with your Wheelluck account — such as usage limits, plan changes, or dashboard confusion — this guide will walk you through the most common issues and how to resolve them.

Common Dashboard Issues

If you're having trouble with the dashboard:

  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try accessing from a different browser
  • Check your internet connection
  • Contact support if issues persist
