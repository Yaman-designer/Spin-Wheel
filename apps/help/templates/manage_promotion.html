How to Manage Promotions

Validated on 15 Jan 2024 • Last edited on 27 Jun 2025

Overview

Learn how to create, update, and delete promotions in your Wheelluck dashboard. Promotions allow you to create engaging spin-to-win campaigns for your customers.

Creating a Promotion

  1. Log in to your Wheelluck dashboard.
  2. Navigate to the Promotions section.
  3. Click the NEW PROMOTION button.
  4. Fill in the promotion details:
    • Promotion name
    • Website
  5. Click Continue to create your promotion.

Updating a Promotion

  1. Go to the Promotions section in your dashboard.
  2. Find the promotion you want to edit.
  3. Click on the promotion to expand its options.
  4. Click the Edit button.
  5. Modify the promotion details, settings, or design as needed.
  6. Review your changes and click Save to update.
  7. Your changes will be applied immediately to your live promotion.

Deleting a Promotion

  1. Navigate to the Promotions section.
  2. Find the promotion you want to delete.
  3. Click on the promotion to expand its options.
  4. Click the Delete button.
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted.
  6. The promotion and all associated data will be permanently removed.

Important Notes

  • Creating: Make sure to test your promotion before going live.
  • Updating: Changes to active promotions take effect immediately.
  • Deleting: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All data and statistics will be lost.
  • Always backup important promotion data before making significant changes.
  • Review the promotion on your site and in the dashboard before deleting if you need to keep any external copies.
