How can I add a background image to my promotion?

Validated on 15 Jan 2024 • Last edited on 27 Jun 2025

Overview

Adding a background image to your promotion can make it more visually appealing and help it stand out. However, background image uploads are only available when using the "Create Custom Theme" template. This guide will show you how to upload, select, and manage background images for your promotion.

Important: Template Requirement

Note: Background image uploads are only available when you have selected "Create Custom Theme" from the Templates dropdown. If you're using a pre-designed template, the background image section will be disabled.

To enable background image uploads:

  1. Go to the Templates section in your promotion editor.
  2. Click on the template dropdown menu.
  3. Select "Create Custom Theme" from the list.
  4. The Background Image section will now be enabled and ready to use.

Accessing Background Image Settings

  1. Log in to your Wheelluck dashboard.
  2. Navigate to the Promotions section from the left-hand menu.
  3. Click on the promotion you want to edit, or create a new promotion.
  4. Make sure you have selected "Create Custom Theme" from the Templates dropdown.
  5. On the promotion editing page, locate the Background Image accordion section.
  6. Click on the Background Image accordion to expand it and reveal the image gallery.

Image Requirements

Before uploading an image, make sure it meets the following requirements:

Supported File Formats

  • JPEG (.jpg, .jpeg)
  • PNG (.png)
  • WEBP (.webp)
  • GIF (.gif)
  • SVG (.svg)

File Size Limit

  • Maximum file size: 1 MB (1024 KB)
  • If your image is larger than 1 MB, you'll need to compress it before uploading.

Image Count Limit

  • You can upload a maximum of 5 background images per promotion.
  • Once you reach 5 images, the upload placeholder will disappear, and you won't be able to upload more images until you delete one.
Tip: For best results, use high-quality images that are optimized for web use. Consider using image compression tools to reduce file size while maintaining visual quality.

Uploading a Background Image

  1. In the Background Image section, you'll see an image gallery area.
  2. If you have fewer than 5 images, you'll see an upload placeholder (a cloud icon with an arrow).
  3. Click on the upload placeholder to open the file selection dialog.
  4. Browse your computer and select the image file you want to upload.
  5. The image will be automatically uploaded and added to your gallery.
  6. Once uploaded, the image will appear in the gallery as a thumbnail.
  7. The upload placeholder will show a tooltip with format and size requirements when you hover over it.
Automatic Upload: Once you select an image file, it will be uploaded immediately. You don't need to click a separate "Upload" button.

Selecting a Background Image

After uploading images, you need to select which one you want to use as your promotion's background:

  1. In the image gallery, you'll see thumbnails of all uploaded images.
  2. Click on the image you want to use as the background.
  3. The selected image will be highlighted with a border or selection indicator.
  4. The background image will be immediately applied to the promotion preview on the right side of the screen.
  5. You can click on different images to switch between them and see how they look.
Preview: Always check the preview panel to see how your background image looks with your promotion design. Make sure text remains readable and the image doesn't interfere with important elements.

Deleting a Background Image

If you want to remove an image from your gallery:

  1. In the image gallery, locate the image you want to delete.
  2. Click on the trash icon (🗑️) in the top-right corner of the image thumbnail.
  3. The image will be immediately removed from the gallery and deleted from your promotion.
  4. If you had 5 images and deleted one, the upload placeholder will reappear, allowing you to upload a new image.
Permanent Deletion: Deleting an image is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure you really want to delete it before clicking the trash icon.

Removing the Background Image

If you want to remove the background image from your promotion (but keep the images in the gallery):

  1. Look for the Reset button in the Background Image section (if available).
  2. Alternatively, you can simply not select any image, or the system may provide a reset option.
  3. The background will revert to the default background color or no background image.
Note: Removing the background image from use doesn't delete the images from your gallery. You can select them again later if needed.

Best Practices

  • Image Quality: Use high-resolution images that look good when displayed as backgrounds. Avoid images that are too busy or have too many details that might distract from your promotion content.
  • File Size: Optimize your images before uploading to ensure they're under 1 MB. This helps with faster loading times.
  • Contrast: Choose background images that provide good contrast with your text and promotion elements. Dark images work well with light text, and vice versa.
  • Brand Consistency: Use images that match your brand identity and website design.
  • Readability: Ensure that all text and important elements remain readable when the background image is applied.
  • Testing: Always preview your promotion with the background image to make sure everything looks good before saving.

Important Notes

  • Template Requirement: Remember that background images are only available with "Create Custom Theme". If you switch to a pre-designed template, your background images will be disabled, but they won't be deleted from your gallery.
  • Save Your Changes: Always remember to click the Save button after uploading or selecting background images to ensure they are saved permanently.
  • Image Limit: You can upload up to 5 images, but only one can be active as the background at a time.
  • Preview Updates: Changes to background images are immediately reflected in the preview panel, so you can see how they look in real-time.
  • Background Color: When a background image is applied, it will overlay any background color settings. The background color may still be visible if the image has transparency.

Troubleshooting

Background Image Section Is Disabled

  • Check which template is currently selected in the Templates dropdown.
  • If you're using a pre-designed template (not "Create Custom Theme"), background image uploads are disabled.
  • Switch to "Create Custom Theme" to enable background image uploads.
  • You may see a tooltip message: "To upload and use a background photo, please use the Default Template."

Upload Fails with "Image size cannot exceed 1MB"

  • Your image file is larger than 1 MB.
  • Use an image compression tool to reduce the file size.
  • Consider reducing the image dimensions or quality to meet the size requirement.
  • Try converting the image to a more efficient format (e.g., WEBP) if possible.

Upload Fails with "Only JPEG, PNG, JPG, WEBP, GIF, SVG formats are allowed"

  • Your image file is in an unsupported format.
  • Convert your image to one of the supported formats: JPEG, PNG, JPG, WEBP, GIF, or SVG.
  • Most image editing software can convert images to these formats.

Cannot Upload More Images (Upload Placeholder Disappeared)

  • You've reached the maximum limit of 5 images per promotion.
  • Delete one or more existing images from the gallery to free up space.
  • Click the trash icon on an image you no longer need.
  • The upload placeholder will reappear once you have fewer than 5 images.

Image Not Appearing in Preview

  • Make sure you clicked on the image thumbnail to select it as the background.
  • Check that the image was successfully uploaded (it should appear in the gallery).
  • Try clicking on the image again to ensure it's selected.
  • Refresh the page if the preview doesn't update.

Cannot Delete Image

  • Make sure you're using "Create Custom Theme" template.
  • In pre-designed templates, image deletion is disabled.
  • Switch to "Create Custom Theme" to enable deletion.
  • Try clicking directly on the trash icon, not the image itself.

Image Upload Takes Too Long or Fails

  • Check your internet connection.
  • Ensure the image file size is under 1 MB.
  • Try uploading a smaller or more optimized image.
  • Check if there are any browser console errors (press F12 to open developer tools).
  • Try refreshing the page and uploading again.
