How do I change my password?

Validated on 15 Jan 2024 • Last edited on 27 Jun 2025

Overview

Changing your password regularly is an important security practice. You can update your password at any time from your account settings. This guide will walk you through the process step by step.

Accessing the Password Change Page

  1. Log in to your Wheelluck dashboard.
  2. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner of the page.
  3. Select Change Password from the dropdown menu.
  4. You'll be taken to the Password Change page.

Changing Your Password

On the Password Change page, you'll need to fill in the following fields:

  1. Old Password:
    • Enter your current password
    • This is required to verify your identity before changing the password
    • You can click the eye icon to show/hide the password while typing
  2. New Password:
    • Enter your new password
    • You can click the eye icon to show/hide the password while typing
    • Make sure your new password meets all the requirements (see below)
  3. Confirm New Password:
    • Re-enter your new password to confirm it
    • This must match exactly with the "New Password" field
    • You can click the eye icon to show/hide the password while typing

After filling in all fields, click the Change Password button to save your new password.

Password Requirements

Your new password must meet the following security requirements:

  • Minimum Length: At least 8 characters long
  • Uppercase Letter: Must contain at least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
  • Lowercase Letter: Must contain at least one lowercase letter (a-z)
  • Number: Must contain at least one digit (0-9)
  • Special Character: Must contain at least one special character (e.g., !, @, #, $, %, etc.)
  • Different from Current: Your new password must be different from your current password
Example of a valid password: "MySecure123!" (contains uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special character, and is 12 characters long)

What Happens After Password Change

  1. After successfully changing your password, you'll see a success message.
  2. You'll be automatically logged out of your current session for security purposes.
  3. You'll receive a confirmation email at your registered email address notifying you that your password has been changed.
  4. You'll need to log in again with your new password.
Security Notice: If you did not initiate this password change, please contact support immediately to secure your account.

Tips for Creating a Strong Password

  • Use a combination of letters, numbers, and special characters
  • Make it memorable but not easily guessable (avoid personal information like names or birthdays)
  • Consider using a passphrase with mixed characters (e.g., "MyDog!2024#Secure")
  • Don't reuse passwords from other accounts
  • Change your password periodically (every 3-6 months is recommended)
  • Never share your password with anyone

Troubleshooting

  • Old password is incorrect: Make sure you're entering your current password correctly. Check for typos and ensure Caps Lock is off if needed.
  • New password doesn't meet requirements: Review the password requirements above and ensure your password meets all criteria (8+ characters, uppercase, lowercase, number, special character).
  • Passwords don't match: Make sure the "New Password" and "Confirm New Password" fields contain exactly the same text. Check for any extra spaces or typos.
  • New password same as old password: Your new password must be different from your current password. Choose a different password.
  • Cannot see password fields: Use the eye icon next to each password field to toggle visibility. This helps you verify what you're typing.
  • Forgot your current password: If you've forgotten your current password, you'll need to use the "Forgot Password" feature to reset it instead of changing it.
  • Not receiving confirmation email: Check your spam/junk folder. If you still don't receive it, wait a few minutes and check again. Contact support if the issue persists.

Important Security Notes

  • Email Notification: You'll always receive an email notification when your password is changed. This helps you detect unauthorized access.
  • Session Logout: After changing your password, you'll be logged out of your current session. This is a security feature to ensure only you can use the new password.
  • Multiple Devices: If you're logged in on multiple devices, you'll need to log in again with your new password on all devices after changing it.
