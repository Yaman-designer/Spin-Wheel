Overview of Notifications at Wheelluck

At Wheelluck, we keep you informed about your account, service updates, and promotional opportunities. This guide outlines the various types of communications you may receive from Wheelluck and how you can manage them to best suit your preferences.

Types of Communications You May Receive

Wheelluck sends various types of notifications to keep you updated and to help you manage your campaigns:

  • Essential Service & Transactional Communications: These include important updates regarding your account, service functionality, security alerts, and administrative notices. These communications are essential for the proper functioning of your Wheelluck account and services, and generally cannot be opted out of.
  • Marketing & Promotional Emails: We may send you commercial or promotional emails and newsletters to inform you about new features, special offers, webinars, or other products and services that might be of interest to you.
  • Policy & Security Updates: In case of any material changes to our Privacy Policy, Terms of Use, or other security-related matters, we will notify you via email to your registered address or through push notifications on the Website to ensure you are always informed.

Managing Your Notifications

You have control over certain types of communications you receive from Wheelluck:

  • Opting Out of Marketing Emails: You can easily opt out of receiving commercial or promotional emails and newsletters from Wheelluck at any time. Simply click the "UNSUBSCRIBE" link located at the bottom of any marketing email you receive from us. Please allow up to 10 days for your request to be processed.
  • Push Notifications: If you receive push notifications from Wheelluck for policy changes or important updates, you can usually manage these preferences through your device's operating system settings or your browser's notification settings.

Please remember that essential service and transactional emails are crucial for maintaining your account and ensuring service continuity, and therefore cannot be opted out of.
