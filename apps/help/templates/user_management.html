How can I update my profile information?

Validated on 15 Jan 2024 • Last edited on 27 Jun 2025

Overview

Keeping your profile information up to date ensures accurate communication and a personalized experience on Wheelluck. You can update your personal information, company details, and email address from your Profile settings page.

Accessing Your Profile Settings

  1. Log in to your Wheelluck dashboard.
  2. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner.
  3. Select Profile or Account Settings from the dropdown menu.
  4. You'll be taken to your Profile page, which has two main tabs:
    • Overview: Update your personal and business information
    • Email: Change your email address

Updating Profile Information (Overview Tab)

In the Overview tab, you can update the following information:

  1. Full Name:
    • Enter your full name as it should appear on your account
    • Important: Full name can only contain alphabetic characters (letters) and spaces - no numbers or special characters
    • Example: "John Smith" ✓ | "John123" ✗ | "John-Smith" ✗
  2. Company:
    • Enter your company or organization name
    • This helps identify your business context
  3. Market:
    • Select your industry or market category from the dropdown menu
    • Choose the category that best describes your business sector (e.g., E-Commerce, Retail, Software, etc.)
  4. Business:
    • Enter a description of your business or role
    • This is a text area where you can provide additional context about your business

After making your changes, click the Save Changes button to update your profile.

Changing Your Email Address

  1. Go to your Profile settings page.
  2. Click on the Email tab.
  3. You'll see your current email address (displayed as read-only).
  4. Click the Change email button to reveal the email change form.
  5. Enter your new email address in the provided field.
  6. Click Save Changes.
  7. Important: You'll receive a verification email at your new address.
  8. Check your new email inbox and click the verification link in the email.
  9. Your email address will only be updated after you verify it.
  10. If you don't receive the verification email, you can click Resend Verification to send it again.
Note: Your email modification requests will only become active after verification. Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see the verification email.

Important Information

  • Full Name Validation: Your full name must contain only letters (a-z, A-Z) and spaces. Numbers, special characters, or symbols are not allowed.
  • Email Verification: Email changes require verification. Your new email won't be active until you verify it through the confirmation email.
  • Email Uniqueness: Each email address can only be associated with one account. If you try to use an email that's already registered, you'll receive an error message.
  • Profile Updates: Changes to your profile information (name, company, market, business) are saved immediately after clicking "Save Changes".
  • Email Updates: Email changes require an additional verification step and are not immediate.

Troubleshooting

  • Cannot save full name: Make sure your name contains only letters and spaces. Remove any numbers, hyphens, or special characters.
  • Email verification not received:
    • Check your spam/junk folder
    • Wait a few minutes and try clicking "Resend Verification"
    • Make sure you entered the correct email address
    • Check if your email provider is blocking emails from Wheelluck
  • Email already in use: The email address you're trying to use is already associated with another account. Try using a different email address or contact support if you believe this is an error.
  • Changes not saving: Make sure you clicked the "Save Changes" button. If issues persist, refresh the page and try again.
  • Profile page not loading: Make sure you're logged in and have the correct permissions. Try logging out and logging back in.
